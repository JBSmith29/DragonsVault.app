Accessibility Statement

Last updated: {{ last_updated }}

DragonsVault is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to conform with Section 508 and WCAG 2.1 AA guidance.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic markup and ARIA attributes on interactive controls.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation and focus management.
  • High-contrast themes and text alternatives for meaningful images.

Ongoing Improvements

We perform periodic audits and welcome feedback that can help us improve accessibility. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know and we will address it as quickly as possible.

Contact

Email the site administrator with “Accessibility Feedback” in the subject line and include a description of the issue as well as your contact information.
