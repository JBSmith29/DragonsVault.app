DragonsVault uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, and measure performance. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your choices.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They allow us to recognize your browser and remember information such as login state, language preferences, or items in progress.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for authentication and core security.
  • Preference cookies – remember display choices such as theme or pagination settings.
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These parties are contractually required to safeguard your information and use it only for the contracted purpose.

Managing Cookies

You can accept or decline optional cookies using the controls presented in the cookie banner, and you may also disable cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may impact certain features, such as staying signed in.

Do Not Sell / Share

California residents can exercise their rights via the Do Not Sell and Do Not Share pages.
